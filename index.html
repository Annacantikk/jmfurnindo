About Us

PT. JAYA MAKMUR INDONESIA ( PT. JMI) is one of the companies which engaged in children's toys business. It was founded with purpose is “To build Indonesian children to be intelligent and creative also can learn independently since early age” The methods is learning through toys. Our company is focus on offering children's toys especially dolls.We give the best effort to fulfill our children of nation needs so they can have an education and creative with great quality product and affordable prices. Therefore, we are continuing make and design dolls at prices. Since the Indonesian government has implemented the Indonesian National Standard (SNI) on all's toys, we always provide the quality of toys in accordance with the K3L (Safety, Security, Health and Environment) concept.
